About the C.T. Bauer College of Business

The Bauer College of business is one of the finest institutions in the nation. Housed at the University of Houston, it hosts the nations best Entrepreneurship program, an outstanding sales program, and a supply chain program ranked #14 by Gartner. The college is home to over 6,000 students and is the most diverse institution in the world.
